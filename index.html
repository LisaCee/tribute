Prince Rogers Nelson

1958 - ∞

Prince was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota, and discovered his gift with music at a young age. He signed a recording contract with Warner Bros. at the age of 18.

Prince wrote, produced and played nearly all of the instruments for his 39 studio albums, inluding the Academy Award winning Purple Rain song score.

Prince also wrote and starred in 4 theatrical releases, including the 1987 concert film Sign 'o the Times

Prince lived many lifetimes in his mere 57 years and will be missed by all who loved him and his music
